About Mamidi HealthTech Solutions

We develop AI-powered software solutions to transform rare disease diagnosis, making it faster, more accurate, and affordable. Our tools support clinicians by identifying genetic variants and predicting outcomes, reducing diagnostic uncertainty for patients.

Our Mission

To empower clinicians and researchers with innovative AI-driven tools for rapid, accurate, and affordable rare disease diagnosis, transforming the diagnostic journey for patients and improving access to timely, personalized care.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in rare disease diagnostics, bridging the gap between advanced technology and patient care. We envision a world where no rare disease patient experiences delays or uncertainty in diagnosis, fostering a future of equitable, precision-driven healthcare for all.

Our Core Values

Patient First

Ensuring precise and reliable diagnostic results

Accessibility

Making advanced diagnostics available to all
